# Eduversa

<section className="text-lg text-muted-foreground mb-6">
A structured platform engineered to eliminate operational chaos in educational institutions.

</section>

---

## What It Is

Eduversa is a full-stack education management system designed to reduce manual work, enforce data consistency, and automate routine academic operations.  
Built for environments where reliability and traceability matter more than aesthetics.

---

## Core Capabilities

- **Attendance Scanner System** – Automated identification and real-time presence tracking  
- **Student Ledger** – Centralized storage of academic and personal records  
- **Administration Layer** – Role-based access control and system governance  
- **Dynamic Forms Engine** – Structured and institution-specific data collection  
- **AI Chat Agent** – Dialogflow-powered assistant for instant system interaction  

---

## Architecture

**Frontend**
- Next.js (server-rendered React)
- SCSS for modular styling

**Backend**
- Node.js runtime
- Express.js REST services

**Automation**
- Dialogflow for conversational logic and intent resolution
